Foldingnet: Point cloud auto-encoder via deep grid deformation, CVPR 2018, Spotlight
Yaoqing Yang, Chen Feng, Yiru Shen, and Dong Tian

Proposed a novel framework for 3D point cloud reconstruction using deep auto-encoder. Our method is simple and efficient, and achieves state-of-the-art for linear transfer classification in ModelNet40 dataset (6/19/2018).
